Adventures Not Tours


Adventures are more than tours!

Dear Customers,

You may see the words "adventures or "expeditions" on our website as much as you see asteroids in the Kuiper Belt. All of our trips are truly adventures, as in you and your group will be dropped off with a lot of resources and a basic home(depending on housing plan). While the resources have enough food and water(other drinks as well), the resource package will contain materials to upgrade your home or to build a new one! You will be left alone with your group to mostly fend for yourselves, We'll be watching you and your group closely for safety purposes, but you'll still have privacy in areas like inside the main home. You'll be safe while still having the thrill of adventure! If worse comes to worse, you'll be given an emergency evac button, give it a press, and Astro Adventures will be right there. With Astro Adventures, you can enjoy a true adventure on unfamiliar terrain like our fore fathers, but without the risk of death like they had!
