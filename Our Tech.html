Our Tech


Safety has reached a new frontier.

Our tech has been fine tuned and tested against even the most dangerous and abrasive areas, and it has passed every test we've put it though. From the gravitational dominance of the Gaia BH1 Black hole, to the seemingly endless navigation required by the Kuiper belt, our AI and propulsion systems have proven time and time again that the limitations that have stopped us before can't stop us again. Good magicians never reveal their secrets, and our tech is nothing short of magic, but we can let you know that you're in good hands.



Vehicles:

Our agency will use advanced spacecraft and technology to transport our customers safely to and from the gas giants. The spacecraft is to be designed with the latest safety features, including radiation shielding and life support systems. The agency would also need to use advanced navigation technology to navigate the complex gravity fields around the gas giants.
